Helpful tips for farmers and providers

The clearer the request, the easier it is to match quickly and safely.

For farmers
  • Write the exact job: ploughing, spraying, harvesting, transport, or maintenance.
  • Add the farm location and timing so providers know whether they can reach you.
  • Check applicant phone numbers and price before agreeing to the work.
For providers
  • Apply only if you can truly do the work in the stated area and time.
  • Share your working phone number and a fair, easy-to-understand price.
  • Use a short message to explain your experience or equipment.
